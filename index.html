I'm a recent graduate from Santa Clara University (SCU). I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering. For two years I was a research assistant in the Human Interaction and Innovation Robotics Lab at SCU.

We developed a mobile manipulator interfaced through speech; check out videos of the robot in action on the projects page and our publications on the publications page

After graduating with my engineering degree cum laude, I traveled outside of the U.S. for the first time to Japan.

While in Japan I tried matcha, a shade-grown green tea powder. I quickly realized that the quality of their matcha was significantly better than anything I've tried in the U.S.

After returning to the U.S., I decided to start an eccomerce business importing matcha from Japan with my business partner Skylar. After testing over 50 different types of matcha from different regions and cultivars, we selected the best matcha we found and developed a strong relationship with our tea farmer in Japan.

We now sell the best matcha on our website mattsmatcha.com.
